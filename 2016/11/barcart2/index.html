Barcart

2016-11-19

By Jacob Berg

The trees
Bar-cart
Tall and thin
Beanpoles
Rise in neat rows
Neat rows scattered
Drifting ever upwards
Just to be sure
You can’t see the other side
The put a forest
So you can’t see
what they’ve hidden
I don’t know where I am
I do know where I’m going
I’m doing nothing
I keep moving forwards
Good afternoon ladies and gentlemen
The bar-cart is between
The first two cars
