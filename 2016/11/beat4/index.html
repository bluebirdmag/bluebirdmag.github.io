Beat

2016-11-19

By Jacob Berg

Fire in the jungle
Of the vacation home timeshare
Bullshit clock twists
Distant lights and long forgotten dances
From the recent past
Tumble across the walls of grey skulls
Grey skulls
Grey skulls
And yellow hearts
Red hearts
Travelling through time
Space
And the world cup
Midway through July in
A laundry basket
Two bags of dirty clothing
350 Karunas
and a sign that said
don’t wash your
clothes here this is for
angry men and
frozen women
whose limbs would shatter
at a hammer’s tap
along the floor
