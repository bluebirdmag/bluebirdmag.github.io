WE WANT YOUR WRITING

2016-11-20

yo -

after filling out lots of forms, having a bunch of meetings, and doing a whole bunch of other boring logistical shit that you don’t care about, we’re excited to tell you –

we’re finally ready to accept submissions.

we’re looking for all types of art from queen’s students and members of the broader kingston community, but we’ll consider work from anyone anywhere who’d like to submit.

poems, short stories, long stories, photographs, paintings, sketches, and the like will be considered for both online and print publication. films, videos, music, and anything else that can’t be printed will be considered for online publication.

SUBMISSIONS ARE, AS OF THIS MOMENT, OPEN 24/7 FOREVER.

THE SUBMISSION DEADLINE FOR OUR INAUGURAL PRINT ISSUE IS DECEMBER 31, 2016

to everyone with a sonnet, a story, a sketch, a song, locked in a cage //
there’s a bluebird in everyone’s heart. let yours out.

bluebird@clubs.queensu.ca

– jb, tb, pm, am, wc, al
