Dirty Hands

2016-11-20

By Editors

flicked on the radio heard the
old man tell me
everything’s gonna be alright and
I found that real funny since
my nana’s about
his age and she’s all
ash I caught her blowing in the wind once which was
real funny since I
put her in the ground myself

it’s too bad I was
also dead at the time otherwise I
coulda carried a lot more weight and
I woulda had a good excuse to cry I
never did that back then on account of
I was stone dead too

there must’ve been something in me cuz
I was eating a lot and
my bloodstream was a pharmacy and
I could feel my heart beating but
my head ran cool and I
didn’t feel I
just watched

I was in the ground when I woke up
real funny how much
dirt you get in your fingernails
trying to dig yourself out
I see it even now and my
best friend used to tell me that
she wasn’t supposed to trust boys with
dirt under their fingernails it would
means he’s unclean and
I always figured she was right but
now I know

I stopped washing my hands a while ago
but you never really know when you’re done
there was this spot that I
couldn’t get rid of
no matter how hard I tried
seems to be gone now
not quite sure where I left it
